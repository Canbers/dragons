Based on Tabletop RPG elements, this game allows the player to go on any advneture of their choosing.
The Game Master is powered by OpenAI's GPT models and will react to whatever the player says and does.

The world structure is procedurally generated systematically, but all the descriptions and colouring is done by AI.
Everything generated is persisted in the database for that world, so that the AI is grounded and does not hallucinate previous events and details.
